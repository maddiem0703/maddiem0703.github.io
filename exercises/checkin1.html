What is climate change?

The United Nations describes climate change as "long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns." These shifts can sometimes occur naturally, but recently human activities have been the main cause. The effects of these changes include hotter temperatures, more severe storms, increased drought, a warming and rising oceean, loss of species, not enough food, more health risks, and poverty and displacement. Climate change isn't only harming the environment; It also causes major problems for people and animals.

What Can Be Done?

  • Save energy at home
  • Change your home's source of energy
  • Walk, bike, or use public transportation
  • Consider your means of travel
  • Reduse, reuse, repair, recycle
  • Eat more vegetables, fruits, nuts, and seeds
  • Eat less meat and dairy
  • Waste less food
  • Plant native species
  • Clean up your environment
  • Support more sustainable businesses
  • Educate and speak up
